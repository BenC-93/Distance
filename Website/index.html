Thanks for checking us out.

Distance is about evoking the emotional journey of a long distance relationship through emergent gameplay.

What do you do?

You experience a world of emotions, trials, and experiences.

Divergence/Convergence

Players go through cycles of cooperative and independent play when their worlds come together and separate again.

Goals

Permanently join yours and your partner's world by defeating monsters which represent the trials of long distance relationships.

Team Overtime

We are a creative game design team located in Santa Cruz, California.
Our goal is to build fun and rewarding experiences.
